Pattern 1

Sensor or device data is collected at the edge gateway via HART (wireless/ wired)

Though, the sensor/ device may have cloud interface capabilities, we recommend routing data through edge-gateway

Based on the rules applied at the edge interface, selective data (e.g. processed vibration analysis) or all data (e.g. raw time and frequency domain data) will flow to the Enterprise data Lake via Manufacturing firewall using HTTPS.

This data can further be consumed by any of the existing applications from Layer 4 and above

Clear demarcation of ownership

Ease of device manageability and new device provisioning

Future ready capability of intelligence at edge

Cloud connectivity that the device or sensor may offer, is not being leveraged
