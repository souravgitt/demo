The Lilly IIoT Platform is a system providing bidirectional connection and transfer of data between various business applications and devices located in various parts of the organizations.

The platform consists of:

  • Interfaces between the Lilly IIoT Platform and end devices, edge gateway
  • Information flow between business Applications and data sources (e.g. existing enterprise Data Lake)
  • Information flow protocol ensuring traceability of data transfer
  • Interaction (monitoring and control)
  • Crosscutting concerns, e.g., security and device management.
