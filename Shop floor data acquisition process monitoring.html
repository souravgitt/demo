Fegersheim Digital Plant

  • Phase-I: The intent of this PoC at one of the Lilly sites is to predict time before production unplanned downtimes by monitoring the health of equipment on CGV3 production line without breaking the current production. Process data like Temperature, Vibration (300,000 data points in 10 seconds) etc., are being monitored in real-time, unlike in the past manually through hand-held devices.
  • Lilly here, is using Wireless HART based battery operated devices for collecting data from all 16 vibration sensors to an Emerson HART gateway. The gateway is configurable in the sense, to route specific data to specific Emerson analytical tool i.e. for Machine diagnostics (AMS-MHM) and Device diagnostics (AMS-IDM) respectively. Further, this data is integrated to OSI-PI system.
  • Phase-II: Lilly would define the variables (IoTs, process values) to predict and avoid equipment's breakage based on data collected in Phase I. Also need is to identify and test the best technology that meet Lilly's needs (such as PTC/Python/R technologies).
